FARM ANALYSIS TABLE
Serial No. Types of Farm Analysis Examples/KPIs Data/Information Source What will the Analysis Inform
1. Production Efficiency
  • average Milk production per cow per day
  • Farm total milk production per day/month/year
  • average lactation production per cow
  • Average lactation length
  • Milk production record
  • Animal
  • Identification record
  • Productivity of the whole farm and/or per cow
  • What and how to improve milk production, lactation length
  • Testing the effectiveness of feeding,housing and other management improvements
2. Reproduction efficiency
  • Average age at first calving
  • Conception rate
  • Age at first insemination
  • Average calving interval
  • Fertility record
  • Young stock
  • To access the effectiveness of different breeding methods used
  • Inform the production and reproductive potential of the herd
3. Herd Replacement
  • Average calf growth rate per month
  • Growth rate at weaning
  • Average age at weaning
  • Average age at 1st service/heat
  • Young stock recording
  • Growth monitoring
  • Management practice the farm should follow at different age/condition of young stock
  • Inform evaluation of replacement strategy of the farm
4. Finance profitability
  • Gross/Net income
  • Sales trendof major farm outputs
  • Proportion of feeds cost to total farm expenditure
  • Per unit(It.) production cost of milk
  • Income and expense recording sales
  • Sales recording
  • Milk production recording
  • Inform whether the farmis profitable or not and by how much
  • Sales trends of the farm outputs
  • Which cost item contribute mosst to the farm expense and which source of income/revenue contribute most to the farm income
